Engineering FAQ

Deep-dive into the technical architecture and compliance standards of the EcoTrack network.

How does the edge computing model reduce latency?

Our IoT mesh sensors perform initial data normalization at the edge. This reduces raw data transmission by 85%, ensuring that critical overflow alerts are processed in under 200ms across the entire municipal grid.

What encryption standards are used for municipal data?

All telemetry data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are SOC2 Type II compliant and offer dedicated instances for agencies requiring strict data residency.

Can the platform handle multi-vendor IoT hardware?

Yes. While EcoTrack provides proprietary sensors, our Headless API is hardware-agnostic, supporting standard MQTT and LoRaWAN protocols for seamless integration with existing city infrastructure.

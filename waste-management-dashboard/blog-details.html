1. Wish-cycling

"Wish-cycling" is the practice of tossing questionable items in the recycling bin, hoping they can be recycled. This often leads to entire batches of recycling being sent to the landfill due to contamination. Always check local guidelines.

2. Dirty Containers

Food residue is a major contaminant. A greasy pizza box or a yogurt cup with leftover spoon scrapings can ruin high-quality paper and plastic bales. A quick rinse makes a huge difference.

"The purity of the recycling stream determines its value. Clean materials are commodities; dirty materials are garbage."

Conclusion

By educating your team and adding clear signage, you can significantly increase your diversion rates and lower your waste costs.

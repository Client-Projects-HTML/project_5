Pricing Questions

Answers to common questions about our plans.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your dashboard. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a minimum contract length?

Small Business and Growth plans are month-to-month. Corporate plans may require an annual commitment for custom infrastructure setup.

Do you offer municipal discounts?

Yes, we have special pricing for government and municipal agencies. Please contact our sales team for a custom quote.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine the right bin size?

Our waste consultants will perform a free audit of your operational output to recommend the most cost-effective bin sizes and pickup frequency.

What materials are prohibited?

Hazardous materials, electronics, and medical waste cannot be placed in standard commercial bins. Please see our centralized disposal services for these items.

Can I change my schedule?

Yes, you can adjust your pickup schedule instantly through our Client Portal.
